# AH_UEVR
A VR first persion mod for UEVR when playing Atomic Heart

## Features include:
- Full 1st person 6DOF Motion controls
- Articulated and animated hands
- Physical gestures for melee combat
- Option to attach the UI to head movement
- Weapon reload and grip animations
- Physical gunstock support

## How to use
Both the left and right hands are used extensively in Atomic Heart. The reticle will follow whichever hand is currently the active controlling hand. To switch which hand is actively being controlled, grip with either hand. Once the reticle is following that hand you can then perform that hand's actions (firing a weapon, casting a spell, grabbing an item, etc)
<BR><BR>
Grabbing items and interacting with the world is done with the left hand (your gloved hand). Point your left palm towards the item you want to pick up or toward a storage unit you want to interact with. The reticle will show which direction your left palm is pointing. Note that left palm targetting is different than most games so if you are having trouble picking up items, make sure you are pointing your palm toward the object, not the normal thumb forward controller orientation. When your left palm is correctly pointed at an object, the in-game overlay will show "RB". Any time you see "RB" grip with the LEFT hand, not the right hand.
<BR><BR>
To interact with the NORA skill tree, point your left finger towards the button you want to press. When the laser is hovered over the button, press either Left Trigger or A.
<BR><BR>
To interact with the puzzle that requires you to press several button in a pattern, point your left finger towards the button you want to press. When the laser is hovered over the button, press either Left Trigger or A.
<BR><BR>
To drive a car, once your are inside the car, use the left stick to turn left and right, and the right trigger to go forward and the right grip to go backward
<BR><BR>
To jump, push up on the right stick. To dodge, pull down on the right stick while moving the left stick in the direction you want to go. To use "Jump boost", run forward and push forward on the right stick to jump and then immediately pull back on the right stick to boost.
